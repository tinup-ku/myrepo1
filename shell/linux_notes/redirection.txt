FILE Handles:

0 - stdin
1 - stdout
2 - stderr

-----------------------
stderr goes to stdout
cmd 2>&1

-----------------------
Below will redirect stdout and stderr to a file foo.outerror:

find /usr -name foo -print 1 > foo.outerror 2>&1
---------------------------

To capture a command's STDOUT and discard its STDERR:

$output = `cmd 2>/dev/null`;      

----------------------------
